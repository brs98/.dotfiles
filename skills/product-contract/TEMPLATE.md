## Compatibility test for product bets

Before normal product-bet intake, a proposed bet must pass:

1. **Named primary user:** Serves a queryable segment in a stated situation.
2. **Evidenced obstacle:** Addresses observed friction in the product's value loop.
3. **Outcome:** Measures primary-user capability or behavior, not feature adoption alone.
4. **Ownership:** Preserves the primary user's agency, context, and stopping point.
5. **Product role:** Stays inside the product's responsibilities and boundaries.
6. **Trust:** Preserves privacy, provenance, safety, and appropriate access.
7. **Strategic reason:** Cites the exact contract claim behind “Why now?”.

If a check fails, pass on the bet or amend the contract first. If it is unknown, gather evidence before shaping a solution. This gate does not replace the normal evidence, appetite, metric, and solution-test gates.
